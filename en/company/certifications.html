Certifications

LASIM S.p.A. stands out thanks to its high-quality and globally-recognised ISO (International Organisation for Standardisation) standards.

The use of ISO standards is essential in creating safe, reliable and high-quality products and services. It also helps companies increase productivity by minimising errors and waste.

ISO standards also help companies enter new markets and contribute to the development of global trade in an objective manner. ISO standards serve to safeguard consumers and end users of products and services by ensuring that certified products comply with the internationally-established minimum standards.

LASIM has specifically earned the following certifications:

IATF 16949 2016

Certification issued by IATF

UNI EN ISO 9001

Certification issued by "SAI GLOBAL"

ISO 14001

Certification issued by "QS INTERNATIONAL"
