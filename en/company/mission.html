Mission

Since 1972, Lasim has been striving for customer satisfaction thanks to its innovative approach, its continuous improvement and the dedication of its staff.

WCM, World Class Manufacturing, is the guiding tool for the improvement process.

The managerial structure, inspired by entrepreneurship, places a strong focus on business policies that protect the environment and the safety and ergonomics of the workstations in order to foster a sense of fulfilment and allegiance among the members of the “Lasim Team”.
