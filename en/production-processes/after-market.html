After Market

The LASIM plants produce 7 million jaws each year.

Thanks to our high production capacity, we can meet the demands of the market, both for original equipment of braking systems and after-market products.

Since 1998 we have been guaranteeing the highest quality standards, in compliance with the standards required by the various car manufacturers, through the use of innovative machinery and the experience of highly qualified personnel, starting from the shearing of individual components to the final assembly.
