Alulasim: Components and Frames

Since 2018 LASIM S.p.A. has implemented its industrial skills by introducing processes of moulding and assembly of aluminium components.

The new 3500 m2 industrial building, dedicated exclusively to the processing of this light alloy, has been equipped with new machining centres, such as the folding and bending of profiles, in tandem with a latest-generation 3D laser cutting system with robotic handling, an ultrasonic washing unit and an automated 4-bay MIG welding line for the production of frames and finished components for high-end vehicles.
