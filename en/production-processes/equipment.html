Equipment

The LASIM Equipment Department is specialized in mechanical milling, turning and EDM machining for the construction and maintenance of moulds.

The company can design, manufacture and repair moulds and equipment elements for welding islands, thanks to the use of new numerical control machining centres, machinery always at the forefront combined with the many years of experience of its technicians and workers.

This department represents the essential link with production, ensuring total control of the production flow and quality standards.
