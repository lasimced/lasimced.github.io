Process and Size Checks

The Quality Department controls and formalises the correct execution of the production process, adopting a specific procedure for each phase of the process. For the final decision, three touch probe control machines are used, and a "ROMER" arm for the "point cloud" scanning of the profiles and volumes of the products produced. The quality laboratory, managed by personnel with specific expertise, includes the equipment for the micrographs of the laser welds.
